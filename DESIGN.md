# Design DNA — Wildroot Academy

## Visual Personality: organic-natural
## Layout: community
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Forest Green): #3D5A3E → oklch(0.38 0.07 145)
- Accent (Amber/Terracotta): #C4743A → oklch(0.60 0.12 55)
- Surface (Warm Cream): #FAF5EC → oklch(0.97 0.015 85)
- Text (Deep Bark): oklch(0.22 0.03 85)
- Muted Text: oklch(0.45 0.04 85)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Lora — weight 400-700 (warm, readable, editorial serif for headings)
- Body: Nunito Sans — weight 300-600 (friendly, open, highly readable)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Organic SVG wave dividers** between sections instead of hard horizontal lines — each section flows into the next like terrain
2. **Warm cream + botanical motif hero** with layered radial gradients (amber-gold from top-right, forest green from bottom-left), grain texture overlay — feels like sunlight filtering through autumn leaves
3. **Asymmetric impact stats section** with oversized numerals in forest green and Lora italic pull-quote that breaks the standard grid
4. **Botanical SVG accent ornaments** as section markers — inline leaf/branch illustrations
5. **Card components with warm paper feel** — amber-tinted shadows, generous radius, cream backgrounds — not tech cards, but cozy journal pages
